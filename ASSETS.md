covered by the software licences until a separate asset licence and provenance

## Music and model payloads

Music packs, generated audio, model weights, and the Music Studio runtime are
not part of the MIT/Apache source-code grant. They are intentionally excluded
from Git and may be distributed only under their recorded upstream and output
terms after the release gates in `content/evidence/` and
`docs/content-production-plan.md` are satisfied.
